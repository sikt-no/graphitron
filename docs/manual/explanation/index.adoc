= Explanation
:description: Background on the design decisions, the batching model, and how the pipeline fits together.
:!toc:

Background and rationale, organised by what you might want to understand. None of these pages are required reading; they exist so you can build a mental model when something surprises you, or read up on a design choice before extending past it.

== Architectural framing

* xref:why-database-first.adoc[Why Graphitron is database-first]: the schema, the constraints, and the relationships in your database are the source of truth; the GraphQL layer is a typed view onto them. Cross-links to xref:../../graphitron-principles.adoc[Graphitron principles].
* xref:why-jooq-and-graphql-java.adoc[Why jOOQ and GraphQL-Java]: the two foundational dependencies, why they shape the developer experience, and where each surfaces in your work. Cross-links to xref:../../dependencies.adoc[Dependencies].

== How the build and the runtime fit together

* xref:how-it-works.adoc[How it works]: the pipeline that turns your GraphQL schema and your jOOQ catalog into the resolvers your application serves. Build-time and request-time, in 30 seconds.
* xref:classifier-mental-model.adoc[Classifier mental model]: what the build's classification step does, in user-visible terms. Useful when reading rejection messages or understanding directive interactions.
* xref:batching-model.adoc[The batching model]: why N+1 doesn't appear in Graphitron-generated code, what xref:../reference/directives/splitQuery.adoc[`@splitQuery`] and xref:../reference/directives/lookupKey.adoc[`@lookupKey`] actually do at runtime, and what the N × M contract demands of your custom conditions.

== Why specific things work the way they do

* xref:design-decisions.adoc[Design decisions]: user-visible "why does it work that way" answers for the constraints that look surprising on first contact (why xref:../reference/directives/condition.adoc[`@condition`] methods take a table, why xref:../reference/directives/lookupKey.adoc[`@lookupKey`] blocks pagination, why mutations require xref:../reference/directives/table.adoc[`@table`] on the input type, and more).
